# Reject AI Writing Workflow

1. Identify the artifact type and acceptance bar.
2. Scan for clusters of AI-writing signs, not isolated style preferences.
3. Verify citation integrity, factual specificity, and source alignment before judging style.
4. Discount weak indicators such as perfect grammar, formal prose, blandness, or correct markup.
5. Look for human-writing evidence: timestamp before November 30, 2022, coherent edit rationale, source-backed specificity, or consistent author history.
6. Reject only when strong indicators coincide with unacceptable quality, sourcing, policy, or trust risk.
7. Prefer revision when the text is salvageable through source verification, specificity, and removal of formulaic or fabricated material.
8. Write the outcome with snippets and remediation steps; avoid certainty about authorship unless direct evidence exists.
